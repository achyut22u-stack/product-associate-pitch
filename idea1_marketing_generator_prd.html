The Problem

60dB produces world-class data, but it's often trapped in long PDFs. Clients want "snackable" highlights for their followers, and internal teams spend too much time "clipping" and redesigning these assets manually.

The Solution

A "one-click" internal asset generator. Upload a report, and the AI parses the data, extracts the "Hero Narrative", and populates programmatic brand templates for high-impact LinkedIn Carousels.

The Impact

Increases 60dB's social presence, delights clients with instant "marketing-ready" assets, and frees up analyst capacity by automating 100% of the initial asset design phase.

Implementation Roadmap

01

AI Narrative Mapping

Develop an LLM pipeline that maps report findings to a 7-slide "Story Arc" (The Hook, The Challenge, The Methodology, The Data, The Action, The Outcome, The CTA).

02

Programmatic Canvas

Build 5 high-fidelity HTML/CSS templates based on 60dB's premium website case studies. These templates use the proprietary Union and Maax Mono fonts for strict brand compliance.

03

Headless Rendering

Integrate Playwright to render the populated templates into 1080x1350px PNGs and stitch them into a downloadable PDF package for immediate LinkedIn upload.
