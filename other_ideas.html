Other AI Products I'm Exploring.

Exploring how AI can transform workflows and accelerate impact measurement.

01

Accessible Benchmarking Dashboard

An automated platform that replaces manual database queries with live benchmark mapping and CSV exports. It enables internal teams to sync sector data to projects instantly, while giving clients self-service tools to compare results, calculate performance, and build custom mini-dashboards against global trends.

02

Internal Knowledge Bot

An intelligent search agent for Slack that understands context. Instead of relying on exact keywords, you can describe what you're looking for in natural language. The bot searches channels and threads to find the exact conversation or document you need, even without specific tags.

03

Meeting Next Steps Automator

A meeting assistant that listens to calls to automate documentation. It generates concise Slack messages structured as clear next steps—who, what, and when—automatically tagging relevant people to ensure project momentum is never lost.
