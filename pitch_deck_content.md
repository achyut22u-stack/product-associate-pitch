# Pitch Deck HTML Card Content

This file contains the exact text used in the 8 cards on the HTML pitch deck. Feel free to edit the Titles, Problems, Solutions, or Impacts here. Once you are done editing, let me know, and I will parse this file to automatically update the `pitch_deck.html` file!

## 01. Marketing Asset Generator

**Problem**
PDFs are rigid. Clients want rapid social & blog snippets from their 30-page reports.

**Solution**
A lightweight UI that digests standard reports and outputs Instagram cards, 1-pagers, and LinkedIn text.

**Impact**
Delights clients, saves analyst clipping time, and adds a scalable "freemium" product tier.

---

## 02. Internal Knowledge Base Bot

**Problem**
Years of company intelligence, methodology, and HR rules are buried in Slack histories.

**Solution**
An intelligent Slack assistant trained on internal documentation with instant citations.

**Impact**
Reduces onboarding time, stops repetitive questions directed at ops, and democratizes tribal knowledge.

---

## 03. Meeting Next Steps Automator

**Problem**
Zoom decisions and actions are lost if not meticulously documented by analysts.

**Solution**
A bot hooked to Zoom transcripts that automatically parses and drops summaries into project Slack channels.

**Impact**
Massive accountability boost. Ensures a single source of truth for all project momentum and risks.

---

## 04. Hybrid WhatsApp Survey Flow

**Problem**
Pure phone calls are slow and expensive; standalone WhatsApp surveys suffer from high drop-off.

**Solution**
Automated WhatsApp dispatch and payout logic triggered immediately after a human (or AI) primer call.

**Impact**
Lowers COGS significantly, retains premium data quality, and accelerates the payout process.

---

## 05. Data-to-Deck Populator

**Problem**
Analysts waste hours copy-pasting numbers from Excel to update templatized PowerPoint slides.

**Solution**
A utility that smart-maps dataset columns directly to PPT placeholder formats in seconds.

**Impact**
Eliminates copy-paste errors and frees up analysts to focus strictly on deriving insights.

---

## 06. Automated Deck QA Check

**Problem**
Senior reviewers act as expensive proofreaders catching font, tone, or branding inconsistencies.

**Solution**
An AI "pre-flight" scan validating the deck against the robust 60dB visual and tone guidelines.

**Impact**
Eliminates bottlenecked review cycles, saving high-value Sr. Associate time for strategic refinement.

---

## 07. Contextual Insights Engine

**Problem**
60dB primary data is powerful, but clients need it contextualized instantly against macro-trends.

**Solution**
A split-screen assistant that juxtaposes primary insights against verified macro-data (e.g., World Bank).

**Impact**
Supercharges standard reporting into high-level strategic advisory work, safely avoiding hallucinations.

---

## 08. Automated Metrics Calculator

**Problem**
Calculating core proprietary scores (CPS, PPI) relies on navigating fragile, highly-manual Excel sheets.

**Solution**
A centralized, intuitive web calculator computing all metrics off simple raw data uploads.

**Impact**
Brings speed and historic consistency, replacing clunky files with a robust system of record.
