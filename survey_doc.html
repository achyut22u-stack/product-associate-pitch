60 decibels | Eka Fellowship Impact Survey
Company Context:

The Eka Fellowship is a long-term fellowship programme designed to support young under-privileged children across stages of education. It focuses on career awareness, skills and financial resilience to achieve social mobility. They offer support to the Eka Fellowship students from 9th standard until they get their first job.
