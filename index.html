Try The Live Prototype

Before you test: Your Persona

To experience the AI Enumerator fully, we need to set the scene. This prototype is based on the Eka Fellowship study—a recently completed project as part of the 60 Decibels Inclusive Opportunity.

The Context: The Eka Fellowship is India's first 8-year-long holistic school-to-work fellowship. They identify driven children from underprivileged backgrounds and commit to them from Grade 9 until their first job. They provide holistic growth, career clarity, English language skills, and financial resilience.

Your Role: Pretend you are a parent of an Eka Fellow. Your child has been participating in the program, and you've seen improvements in their English, discipline, and confidence. Answer the AI Enumerator's questions from this perspective.
