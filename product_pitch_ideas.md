# 60 Decibels: Product Associate Pitch Ideas

This document outlines 8 product feature ideas aimed at making employees' lives easier, improving the bottom line, or fundamentally expanding how 60 Decibels works. These concepts align directly with the Product Associate JD, demonstrating a strong understanding of internal workflows, client needs, and practical AI application.

---

## Idea 1: Social Media & Infographic Generator (Client/Internal)
**The Problem:** Clients often request bite-sized, diverse formats (1-page infographics, blog posts, 1-minute videos, social media tiles) derived from the standard 30-page PDF report. Providing this manually is time-consuming.
**The Solution:** A lightweight web app that acts as an asset generator. 
*   **Workflow:** The final report or dataset is fed into the system (or sent via a secure client link). 
*   **Features:**
    *   **Format Selection:** Users choose the desired output (Instagram post, LinkedIn text, blog post, 1-pager).
    *   **AI Summarization:** Extracts key data points and narratives from the report.
    *   **Editability:** An interface allowing the client or internal team to tweak the generated text/images before final download.
**Impact:** Delights clients by giving them immediate marketing assets, saves hundreds of analyst hours, and adds a scalable "freemium" or add-on product tier.

## Idea 2: Internal Knowledge Base Slack Chatbot
**The Problem:** Years of company intelligence, guidelines, methodology discussions, and HR rules are buried in Slack histories or scattered across Notion/Drive.
**The Solution:** An intelligent Slack assistant trained on internal documentation and historical company chats.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Instant Answers:** "Is tomorrow a holiday in the Kenya office?" or "What's our standard tone for the opening slide on gender equity?"
    *   **Source Citations:** Always links back to the original Slack thread or Notion doc for verification.
**Impact:** Drastically reduces onboarding time for new hires, stops repetitive questions directed at senior ops, and centralizes tribal knowledge.

## Idea 3: "Next Steps" Zoom-to-Slack Automation
**The Problem:** Meetings happen over Zoom, but action items, risks, and decisions often get lost if not meticulously documented by an analyst.
**The Solution:** A Slack chatbot integration that automatically hooks into Zoom transcripts.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Post-Meeting Summary:** Instantly drops a summarized list of next steps, decisions made, and pending risks into the relevant project Slack channel.
    *   **Querying:** "What did we decide about the timeline in yesterday's sync?"
    *   **Task Assignment:** Automatically detects who is responsible for what based on transcript context.
**Impact:** Increases accountability, saves project management time, and ensures a single source of truth for project momentum.

---

## Idea 4: Two-Step WhatsApp Survey Automation
**The Problem:** Relying purely on phone calls is resource-intensive. Purely WhatsApp surveys suffer from low response rates or drop-offs without human priming.
**The Solution:** A hybrid, automated outbound system to optimize response rates and respondent payouts.
*   **Features:**
    *   **The Human/AI Primer:** A brief initial call to explain the process and incentivize the respondent (can test AI voice agents like 11Labs here).
    *   **Automated Dispatch:** Triggers the WhatsApp survey sequence immediately after the primer call.
    *   **Payout Integration:** Automatically triggers respondent compensation upon completion.
**Impact:** Lowers the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) for surveys, maintains data quality through human-in-the-loop priming, and speeds up data collection.

## Idea 5: Automated Deck Population Tool
**The Problem:** Analysts spend hours copying and pasting data from internal tools/Excel into templatized PowerPoint decks, updating headings, dates, and values.
**The Solution:** A "data-to-deck" internal utility.
*   **Features:**
    *   **File Upload:** Users drop in the PPT template and the core dataset (Excel/CSV/PDF).
    *   **Smart Mapping:** Automatically maps data column headers to placeholder variables in the PPT.
    *   **Batch Updating:** Refreshes all charts, strings, baseline demographics, and sample sizes in seconds.
**Impact:** Eliminates manual data-entry errors, frees up analysts to focus on *insights* rather than *formatting*, and accelerates project delivery timelines.

---

## Idea 6: Automated Deck QA & Review Engine
**The Problem:** The review process is piecemeal and bottlenecked. Analysts, Consultants, and Senior Associates waste time catching formatting errors, tone inconsistencies, or brand guideline violations.
**The Solution:** An AI-powered "pre-flight" check for client deliverables.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Brand Check:** Scans the deck against 60dB's visual and tonal guidelines (e.g., checking if the right fonts/colors are used, ensuring the empathetic yet rigorous tone is maintained).
    *   **Consistency Check:** Flags mismatched numbers (e.g., sample size on slide 2 mentions 300, but slide 10 says 295) and formatting slips.
*   **Impact:** Ensures that human reviewers (Senior Associates) only spend time reviewing the *value proposition and strategic insights*, not catching typos.

## Idea 7: Contextual Insights Augmented Engine
**The Problem:** 60dB data is incredibly granular, but clients need that data contextualized within broader socio-political, environmental, or economic trends (e.g., comparing farmer yield drops against regional rainfall data).
**The Solution:** A supercharged research assistant for the insight generation phase.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Split-Screen UI:** Left side shows 60dB's proprietary primary data insights. Right side surfaces relevant external macro-trends.
    *   **Anti-Hallucination Guardrails:** Strict citation rules linking to the World Bank, WHO, or verified academic papers. 
    *   **Insight Augmentation:** Does *not* write the insight, but provides the researcher with corroborated external viewpoints to strengthen their proposal.
**Impact:** Elevates the quality of advisory deliverables, turning 60dB from a "data provider" into a "strategic partner."

---

## Idea 8: Automated Metrics Calculator App
**The Problem:** Calculating core proprietary metrics (Customer Protection Score, Poverty Probability Index) requires navigating templatized Excel sheets manually.
**The Solution:** A clean, intuitive web application serving as a centralized metrics calculation engine.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Data Upload:** Drag and drop the raw project data via CSV/PDF.
    *   **Rapid Processing:** Instantly outputs the 10-12 proprietary scores (CPS, PPI, etc.).
    *   **Project Centralization:** Keeps a historical log of scores per client for easy retrieval and benchmarking.
**Impact:** Replaces clunky, fragile Excel files with a robust software solution, reducing manual error and speeding up the final analytics phase.
