# Idea 4: The AI Enumerator - WhatsApp Survey Automation
**Project Context:** Eka Fellowship Impact Survey

## Core Vision
To build a highly intelligent, empathetic WhatsApp chatbot that acts as an experienced Social Science researcher/enumerator. Unlike a rigid standard survey, this tool interacts with respondents conversationally while strictly adhering to a deterministic underlying questionnaire (the Eka Fellowship Survey Tool).

## Key Capabilities & Responsibilities

1. **Conversational Interviewing:**
   * Does not mechanically spit out questions.
   * Acknowledges respondent answers, builds report, and reacts dynamically just like a human 60dB researcher would.
   * Maintains a clean, smooth, and friendly user experience.

2. **Data Logging & Structuring:**
   * Secretly formats the unstructured conversation into a clean spreadsheet on the backend.
   * **Multiple Choice/Select:** Maps the user's conversational intent strictly to the predefined options in the survey tool.
   * **Open-Ended:** Records subjective insight *verbatim*, exactly as typed in first-person without any summarizing or paraphrasing by the AI.

3. **Active Consistency Verification (Logic Checks):**
   * Remembers the context of the entire conversation.
   * Cross-references demographic/timeline data (e.g., if the user says they were a fellow in 2024–2025 but later references doing a project in 2026).
   * Gently nudges the respondent upon detecting anomalies ("Hey! Did you make a mistake while responding to this? Earlier we mentioned...").
   * Yields significantly higher quality data pipelines.

4. **Deep-Dive Probing:**
   * Nudges the fellow to elaborate on brief answers to capture deep qualitative insights regarding their subjective experience of the fellowship.
